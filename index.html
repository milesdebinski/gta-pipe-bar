Demo - Catch the Pipe

This minigame was inspired by rifle reloading system in Atlas game.

Catch the Pipe is implemented on GTA V Role Play private server and applied to various activities (such as repairing a car or resuscitating other players) that the player would normaly randomly pass or fail. Instead, you have to win the minigame to succeed.
